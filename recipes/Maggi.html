Maggi

Description

Maggi is a quick and delicious instant noodle recipe that’s loved by students, bachelors, and anyone needing a fast meal. It takes just 2 minutes to cook and can be customized with veggies or spices.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet Maggi noodles
  • 1.5 cups water
  • Maggi tastemaker (included in packet)
  • Optional: chopped onions, tomatoes, green chilies

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pan.
  2. Add Maggi noodles and optional veggies.
  3. Add tastemaker spice mix.
  4. Cook for 2–3 minutes until water is mostly absorbed.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
